School Visit of JSS Damagum & GDJSS Damagum

Visit to Junior Secondary School (JSS) Damagum and GDJSS Damagum, where the Chairman discussed urgent repair of borehole facilities for adequate water supply.

Youth Engagement Damagum

Visit by the Youth Integrity Protection Association of Damagum. The Chairman encouraged unity, pledged support and accepted the role of Patron.

Security Meeting Emir of Fune

Preparatory security meeting with traditional rulers and security agencies ahead of the coronation of His Royal Highness, Alh. Dr. Saleh Idriss Ibn Usman, Emir of Fune.

Borehole Projects at Damagum

Construction and rehabilitation of boreholes at key locations including school premises and areas behind Damagum market to improve access to clean water.

Earthworks and road grading carried out within Damagum town to improve accessibility, drainage and transportation for residents.

About the Chairman

Hon. Alhaji Baba Goni Bade is a disciplined and people-oriented leader whose administration prioritizes education, water supply, road infrastructure, security collaboration and youth empowerment across Fune Local Government.
